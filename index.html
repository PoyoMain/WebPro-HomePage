About Me

Hello! I'm Chris! I'm a senior at Georgia State University majoring in Computer Science. This is my homepage which is where all my interests, coursework, and any accomplishments will be.

I enjoy challenging myself to try new things while also refining my current skills as well. Currently I'm doing a lot of game development stuff and that is where my current focus lies, but I've also taken an interest to web development and UI/UX design.

I would like to pursue game development as a full time career in the future. It would be nice to either be a member of an indie studio or start an indie studio, but I would also be happy working for a bigger game company.

My Interests

Puzzles

I really like doing jigsaw type puzzles. I dont do them all that often, but they're pretty calming to do and I can listen to music or have something playing in the background while I do them. Playing puzzle video games is really fun too.

Programming

I do a lot of work in the Unity game engine making video games, whether it be for personal projects or for club. Its fun making all sorts of different projects and designing systems for other people to play and have fun with.

Reading

Although its been a bit busy to do so recently, I enjoy reading. Whether its like a novel, comic, or manga, I like getting invested in them and reading for hours on end. Mystery is my favorite genre, so I generally stick to that for mainly everything I read, but branching out sometimes is nice too.

Movies

I really like watching movies. I have to same preference for mystery movies that I do with books. A few years back I like watched a minimum of 1 movie a day for a year and ended up with 473 movies watched that year. My favorite movies are Wall-E, Wolf Children, Iron Giant, Sixth Sense, and Django Unchained. Silence of the Lambs and Shutter Island are really good too.

Food

I just like to eat. Not really much more to say about this one. Went to a sushi buffet for the first time recently and it ended up being so delicious. It was called Sushi Bros.

Video Games

I like playing video games. Its been too busy to play too many recently but they're really fun to play. Two of my favorites are Hollow Knight and Celeste, both have gotten more than one playthrough from me. I love metroidvanias in general.
